3. WHAT PERSONAL INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

We collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Identity and contact information: Full name, email address, phone number, physical address, SA ID number, passport number, SACE number (for educators).
  • Professional and employment details: Current school/institution, role/position, years of experience, subjects taught, phase, preferred provinces/districts, CV data, work history, education, skills, languages, references.
  • Account credentials: Username, password (stored in hashed form).
  • Payment information: Billing details, payment method information (processed by third‑party payment gateways; we do not store full card numbers).
  • Usage and device data: IP address, browser type, operating system, pages visited, time spent, clickstream data, device identifiers, app version.
  • Communications: Messages sent through our chat feature, support requests, feedback.
  • Location data (if permitted): Approximate location derived from IP address or precise location if you enable GPS for transfer matching.

Special personal information (as defined in POPIA) – such as trade union membership, biometric data, or criminal behaviour – is not intentionally collected. You may choose to include a photo (biometric) on your CV, but that is optional and under your control.

4. HOW DO WE COLLECT YOUR INFORMATION?

  • Directly from you: When you register, build a CV, apply for jobs, send messages, upgrade to a paid plan, or contact support.
  • Automatically: Through cookies, log files, and similar technologies when you use the Platform.
  • From third parties: If you sign in with Google or another OAuth provider, we receive your name and email address. Payment processors (e.g., Paystack) share transaction status with us but not full payment card details.

5. WHY DO WE PROCESS YOUR INFORMATION? (LAWFUL BASIS)

We process your personal information for the following purposes, relying on the lawful bases set out in POPIA (consent, contractual necessity, legitimate interest, or legal obligation):

  • To create and manage your account, provide core features (CV builder, cover letters, job applications, educator matching, chat). – Performance of a contract.
  • To process payments for subscriptions or one‑time purchases. – Performance of a contract.
  • To verify your identity (e.g., optional SA ID verification for CVs). – Consent (you choose to verify).
  • To improve the Platform, analyse usage trends, fix bugs, and personalise your experience. – Legitimate interest.
  • To send service notifications (e.g., subscription renewal, payment confirmation, transfer match alerts). – Performance of a contract / Legitimate interest.
  • To comply with legal obligations (e.g., tax laws, court orders). – Legal obligation.
  • To send marketing communications (only with your explicit consent – you may opt out at any time). – Consent.
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, or illegal activities (e.g., reselling CVs). – Legitimate interest.

6. COOKIES AND TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in.
  • Understand how you use the Platform.
  • Show relevant job recommendations.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

7. SHARING YOUR INFORMATION WITH THIRD PARTIES

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it only in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics, payment processing, email delivery) – To operate the Platform. They are contractually bound to protect your data.
  • Employers / schools (when you apply for a vacancy) – To facilitate job applications. We share only the information you have included in your CV/cover letter.
  • Other users (educators) – In the educator matching feature, your profile (name, school, subjects, phase, etc.) is visible to other educators. Chat messages are visible only to the participants.
  • Law enforcement or regulators – When required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety.
  • Successors (in case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets) – Your information may be transferred as part of the business transaction, subject to the same privacy commitments.

We do not share your personal information with any third party for their own direct marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

8. INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS OF INFORMATION

We store your information on servers located in South Africa (Supabase). If we ever transfer your data to a country outside South Africa, we will ensure that the recipient provides an adequate level of protection (e.g., through binding corporate rules or standard contractual clauses) and that the transfer complies with POPIA.

9. DATA SECURITY

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS) and at rest.
  • Access controls and authentication.
  • Regular security assessments.
  • Staff training on data protection.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

10. DATA RETENTION

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. After you delete your account (or if your account is terminated), we will generally delete your personal information within 30 days, unless we are required to retain it for longer due to:

  • Legal obligations (e.g., tax records for 5 years).
  • Ongoing disputes or investigations.
  • Prevention of fraud or abuse (e.g., data of banned users may be retained for a reasonable period).

CVs, cover letters, and chat messages are deleted when you delete them, but backups may retain copies for a short period.
